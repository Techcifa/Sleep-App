Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Rest & Renewal ("the App", "we", "our") is a personal sleep tracking journal designed to help you understand and improve your sleep habits. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about how we handle your data.

🔒 Your health and sleep data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose outside of providing your personal sleep insights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you explicitly provide or consent to share:

  • Account Information: Your email address for authentication (via Supabase).
  • Sleep Logs: Bedtime, wake time, sleep duration, quality ratings, and optional notes that you enter manually.
  • Habit Tags: Optional lifestyle tags (e.g. Caffeine, Exercise) you attach to your sleep entries.
  • Health Connect Data (Android, optional): With your explicit permission, we read sleep session data from Android Health Connect to automatically fill in your sleep journal. See Section 2 for full details.

2. Android Health Connect – Sleep Data

The App optionally integrates with Android Health Connect to automatically import your sleep data recorded by your phone, smartwatch, or fitness tracker.

What data we access:

  • Sleep session start and end times
  • Sleep stage classifications (light, deep, REM, awake) when available

How we use this data:

  • To automatically create sleep journal entries on your behalf, saving you manual effort.
  • To calculate sleep statistics and trends visible only to you inside the App.

What we do NOT do with your health data:

  • We do not sell or share your health data with any third party.
  • We do not use your health data for advertising or profiling.
  • We do not access Health Connect data in the background without your explicit action.
  • We do not store raw Health Connect data on our servers beyond what you see in your journal.

Health Connect permissions are entirely optional. You can revoke them at any time by opening Settings → Apps → Health Connect → App Permissions → Rest & Renewal on your Android device. Revoking access does not delete already-imported journal entries.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your sleep journal entries are stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database provider, using row-level security. Only your authenticated account can read or modify your data. We use encrypted HTTPS connections for all data transfer.

4. AI Analysis (Optional)

If you use the AI Sleep Analysis feature, a anonymized summary of your sleep statistics (averages, trends — never raw times or personal identifiers) is sent to the DeepSeek API to generate insights. This summary is not stored by us after the response is received.

5. Data You Can Delete

You can delete individual sleep entries at any time inside the App. To fully delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below and we will process your request within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Rest & Renewal is not directed at individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notice. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
